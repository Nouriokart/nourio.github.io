Information Page

This website has been designed to help users brainstorm and put their visions of their ideal building into action. Whether you're an architect, someone that chooses materials, or just have a passion for buildings, you have tools that can help you create something to be proud of. There are buttons on every page so that should you want to go back, you can. Once you choose to begin designing, click on the Let's Design button on the Home Page and you will be asked to choose a budget. Make sure your budget is reasonable depending on the building you plan on designing as products can have high prices.
